Luxury House for sale in Tuscany

Esterni

Set in Chianti this luxury villa is for sale. The prestigious building is dated around the year 1000: in ancient times it was a watch tower. Built entirely of stone, with a fine paved courtyard where the centre stands an ancient well, the house for sale has been able to skillfully combine antiquity and modernity, originality and sophistication. The Tuscan traditional taste has adapted perfectly to even the most modern comforts: the large interior spaces, equipped and fitted with the most modern services, maintain quality and classic elements. Original terracotta floors, fireplace in the seventeenth century, with vaulted ceilings, elegant and precious furniture, valuable and special styling details worthy of a luxury and exclusive villa: these are some of the winning features of the property of prestige. Externally there is a large park that surrounds the luxury house for sale. A rich vegetation of cypresses, oaks, strawberry trees, aromatic plants and flowers form a picture postcard. A swimming pool stands in the midst of all this green, ideal for moments of relaxation and to enjoy unforgettable
